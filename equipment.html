Court

In some communities, there are dedicated indoor and outdoor courts for pickleball. Sometimes you may even see a tennis court with lines necessary for pickleball play. In some recreation facilities, they may set up pickleball on basketball courts.

Paddle

When selecting your paddle you will need to consider: price, grip size, and weight. Many beginners start playing the game using a wooden paddle, while advanced players may use a paddle made of aluminum. The material and how the paddle is made will help to determine the amount of power and control you will have on your shot.

Ball

In selecting the ball that you need, you will have to consider whether you intend on playing indoors or outdoors. Balls with larger holes are made for indoor use, while balls that are harder and have smaller holes are for outdoor use. The smaller holes should help the ball to not catch the wind.
